# Code of conduct

Be specific, evidence-led, and respectful.

- Critique workflows and artifacts without attacking contributors.
- State uncertainty and conflicts of interest.
- Do not publish private prompts, customer material, credentials, or identifying usage data.
- Respect upstream licenses and attribution.
- Accept that a useful private workflow may still be outside the public product boundary.

Maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations. Report sensitive incidents privately through the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
